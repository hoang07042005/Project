About Rhythm House

Rhythm House is not just a vinyl record store; it's a symphony of passion rooted in Hanoi and harmonizing throughout Vietnam. Born in 2023 from the shared love of music by five young enthusiasts, our mission extends beyond selling vinyl records. We aim to deliver music to you through a multi-sensory experience, engaging your ears, eyes, hands, brain, and heart.

We believe that music transcends mere auditory pleasure; it should be an immersive journey. Rhythm House recognizes the vinyl record as the quintessential medium to preserve the authentic sound and evoke the complete emotional spectrum for the listener. Above all, it stands as a marvelous gift for the soul that cherishes the art of music.
